# Case 06 — Eels (Eelektross) in the Snake row

**Question:** Should Eelektross be restored to Water Snake under market-acceptance test, or does the strict 鳗-vs-蛇 distinction hold?

## The structural problem

In Chinese tradition, eels are 鳗 (mán), written with the fish radical 魚. They are categorized as fish, not snakes. 蛇 is reserved for legless reptiles. Per criterion 6 strict, eels orphan from the Snake row.

This was the project's original ruling — Eelektross dropped, Water Snake reduced to Milotic (Feebas-Milotic, sea-serpent visual coding).

## Design lore

Per [Bulbapedia: Eelektross](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Eelektross_(Pok%C3%A9mon)): Electric, EleFish Pokémon. "Eelektross resembles a long, snake-like deep-sea creature with a mostly black body... with the appearance of an eel, lamprey, or moray eel." So Bulbapedia explicitly classifies Eelektross by both eel and serpentine descriptors.

Pure Electric type — element-orphan per R5 anyway.

## Market evidence — TPC's actual Year of the Snake 2025 collection

Per [Pokémon Center — Lunar New Year Year of the Snake 2025 collection](https://www.pokemon.com/us/pokemon-news/celebrate-the-lunar-new-year-with-pokemon-center), TPC officially shipped these as Year-of-the-Snake Pokémon:

- **Snivy** (Grass — Wood Snake)
- **Dudunsparce** (Normal — Earth Snake)
- **Giratina Origin Forme** (Ghost/Dragon — *not snake-typed at all*, included for serpent visual)
- **Gorebyss** (Water — sea-serpent fish, *not snake-typed*)
- **Huntail** (Water — sea-serpent fish, *not snake-typed*)
- **Onix** (Rock/Ground — Earth Snake / Metal Snake cross)
- **Dunsparce** (Normal — Earth Snake)
- **Ekans** (Poison — Earth Snake under R5 terrestrial-Poison extension)
- **Pikachu** (zodiac mascot in Lunar New Year costume — universal cameo)

**Critical observation:** TPC included Gorebyss and Huntail (both Water-typed sea-serpent fish, NOT 蛇 by Chinese strict reading) AND Giratina Origin Forme (Ghost/Dragon serpent). The publisher's actual zodiac merchandising honors **visual coding**, not Chinese strict-character distinction.

If Gorebyss / Huntail / Giratina pass for Year of the Snake, Eelektross — which Bulbapedia describes as "long, snake-like deep-sea creature" — would absolutely pass the same market test.

## Element fit

Pure Electric. Per R5, pure Electric is element-orphan (Wu Xing has no Air phase). This is the harder problem than the animal-fit for Eelektross.

Even if we extend R6 to accept eels as snakes (per market test), the Wuxing element layer still doesn't fit. Eelektross can't anchor *any* element row because Electric doesn't map.

The grid's element-row constraint is the hard limit here. The market test handles animal-fit but not element-fit when the typing is Wuxing-orphan.

## Recommendation

**Conditional restoration.** Two paths:

**Path A — strict Wuxing element preserved:** Eelektross stays orphaned for the grid (no element row), but the page should acknowledge it via a footnote: "TPC's Year of the Snake 2025 lineup included Eelektross-tier sea-serpent fish (Gorebyss, Huntail) and Giratina by visual coding. The grid's strict R5 element rule prevents pure Electric from anchoring; if you're building a personal Year-of-the-Snake box, the market would absolutely accept Eelektross — it just doesn't have a Wuxing-element home in the grid." This preserves R5 integrity.

**Path B — extend R5 to allow Electric-with-water-habitat → Water:** Eelektross lives underwater, eel/lamprey-coded, sea-creature ecology. Could extend by analogy to "Poison + terrestrial habitat → Earth" extension. Eelektross-as-Water-Snake under "Electric + aquatic habitat → Water" would let it land in Water Snake. Risky — opens "Pikachu in some Water cell" arguments. Probably too permissive.

**Status:** Path A recommended. Eelektross stays orphaned for the grid, with a clarifying footnote. Water Snake stays Milotic-only (depth 2).
