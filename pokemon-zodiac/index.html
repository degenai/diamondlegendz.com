Why so many open cells? — Tier 3 footnotes

Open cells are the most interesting cells. They show where the Pokémon dex went Western, Japanese, or fantastical instead of reaching into Chinese tradition. Honored gaps, not failures.

Domestic cats — zodiac-orphaned per the Great Race myth (Rat tricked Cat off the Ox; cat-rat enmity is canonical). Persian, Espeon, Skitty, Litten line, Sprigatito, Meowth all slot-orphaned.

Foxes (狐仙) — supernatural fox-spirit lineage in Chinese folklore, distinct from domestic-dog category. Vulpix, Ninetales, Zorua/Zoroark, Fennekin/Delphox, Nickit, Vaporeon all slot-orphaned.

Wolves (狼) — wild predators, antagonist of the shepherd in agrarian symbolism, not a domestic-companion canid. Mightyena, Lycanroc, Zacian/Zamazenta all slot-orphaned.

Lions (狮) — Buddhist-imported iconography (guardian-lion statues, lion dance from Indian/Central Asian Buddhism), not native Chinese fauna. Pyroar, Solgaleo, Entei, Suicune, Hisuian Arcanine all slot-orphaned. Tiger row goes fully open as a result — the dex has lots of "big cats" but no actual tigers.

Most birds — Rooster slot is chicken-specific in mainstream Chinese tradition; the Pokémon dex's massive avian variety is a structural mismatch with a zodiac that allotted one bird slot to the domestic chicken. Skarmory, Dodrio, all raptors/songbirds/waterfowl/owls slot-orphaned.

Pure Electric — Wu Xing has no Air element. Lightning is air-coded in Pokémon (Zapdos Electric/Flying, Tornadus/Thundurus paired wind-lightning genies). Wu Xing's Metal is substantive (refinement, structure, blade), not kinetic. Pikachu, Mareep line, Pawmi, Yamper line all element-orphaned.

Pure Flying / Psychic / Dark / Fairy / Ghost — no Five-Phases equivalent. Element-orphaned even when animal-slot eligible.

Western dragons — winged fire-breathers, distinct from Chinese loong (serpentine, whiskered, four-clawed, sky/water-associated). Charizard line, Reshiram, Turtonator, Volcarona slot-orphaned despite Dragon-type or fire-bug coding.

Kaiju — Japanese mythological tradition, not Chinese. Tyranitar line slot-orphaned. The Dragon row tightens to Water Dragon only — Kingdra (seahorse-loong), Palkia, Gyarados (the carp-dragon transformation, 鯉躍龍門), Dragalge.

Non-Chinese fauna — zebras (African — Blitzle/Zebstrika), American bison (Bouffalant — wrong continent), jackals (Egyptian/Mediterranean — Lucario), mammoths/hippos/tapirs all slot-orphaned even when type-honest.

Houndoom specifically — orphaned from both the Goat row (Baphomet/European Christian symbolism, not pastoral Chinese) AND the Dog row (demonic hellhound, not a domestic companion).
