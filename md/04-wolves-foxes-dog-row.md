# Case 04 — Wolves and foxes in the Dog row

**Question:** Should Mightyena (Dark hyena), Lycanroc (Rock wolf forms), Vaporeon (fox-coded eeveelution), and Vulpix line (kitsune fox) be restored to the Dog row under the market-acceptance test?

## The structural problem

Chinese culture distinguishes 狗 (gǒu, domestic dog) from 狼 (láng, wolf) and 狐 (hú, fox) — three separate characters carrying separate cultural weight. 狼 represents the wild predator (antagonist of the shepherd in agrarian symbolism); 狐 the supernatural fox-spirit (狐仙 lineage); 狗 the domestic companion. Per criterion 11 strict, only domestic dogs anchor the Dog row.

## Design lore

Per Bulbapedia:
- **[Mightyena](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Mightyena_(Pok%C3%A9mon))** — Dark, Bite Pokémon, based on a hyena (with elements of a wolf). The Poochyena pre-evo is "Bite Pokémon," explicitly hyena-coded.
- **[Lycanroc](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Lycanroc_(Pok%C3%A9mon))** — Rock, Wolf Pokémon. Three forms (Midday, Midnight, Dusk).
- **[Vaporeon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Vaporeon_(Pok%C3%A9mon))** — Water, Bubble Jet Pokémon. Eevee line. Fox / fish-mermaid hybrid design.
- **[Vulpix](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Vulpix_(Pok%C3%A9mon))** — Fire (Kantonian) / Ice (Alolan), Fox Pokémon. Explicitly kitsune-coded.

## Market evidence

**Pokémon GO Year of the Dog 2018 Lunar New Year event** explicitly featured **Poochyena** (pre-evo of Mightyena, hyena/wolf-coded) **and Eevee** (fox-coded eeveelution base) **and Snubbull** (Fairy bulldog) **and Electrike** (Electric dog) **and Growlithe** (Fire dog) as the Year-of-the-Dog spawns. Source: [Pokémon Blog — Year of the Dog 2018 event](https://pokemonblog.com/2018/02/15/you-can-now-celebrate-the-year-of-the-dog-in-pokemon-go-during-the-lunar-new-year-event-featuring-shiny-poochyena-poochyena-growlithe-eevee-snubbull-and-electrike-with-bonus-stardust/) and [Inverse — Year of the Dog Lunar New Year event](https://www.inverse.com/article/41374-pokemon-go-year-of-the-dog-lunar-new-year).

This is The Pokémon Company directly bundling **wolves (Poochyena/Mightyena), foxes (Eevee line), and electric/fairy/fire dogs** as the canonical Year-of-the-Dog Pokémon. They did NOT honor the strict 狗/狼/狐 distinction. The market behavior is overwhelmingly: any canid silhouette qualifies.

Pokémon Center merchandise across multiple Year-of-the-Dog adjacent products has consistently included Eevee, Vulpix, Lucario, and various canid-coded designs. TPC's commercial position is unambiguous: foxes and wolves count as dogs for collector purposes.

## The conflict

Same shape as Case 03 (Western dragons): strict cultural-character distinction is real, but TPC's empirical behavior overrides it for collector purposes.

## Recommendation

**Restore wolves and foxes to the Dog row under market-test exception.** Specifically:

- **Wood Dog:** still likely open (no clean grass-typed dog in dex)
- **Fire Dog:** Arcanine line, Hisuian Arcanine, Houndoom (also see Case 05), Growlithe
- **Earth Dog:** Stoutland (Lillipup line, Normal-as-mundane), Lycanroc (Rock-as-Earth, wolf — restored under market test), Hisuian Arcanine cross-flex, Mightyena (Dark hyena — restored under market test, despite Dark element-orphan concern; passes via "Dark + terrestrial habitat → Earth" by analogy with Poison-extension R5 rule)
- **Metal Dog:** Lucario line, Mega Lucario, Zacian Crowned Sword, Zamazenta Crowned Shield (already restored)
- **Water Dog:** Vaporeon — Eevee-Vaporeon line restored under market test (TPC put Eevee in Year-of-Dog event)

**Caveat on Mightyena:** Pure Dark element is genuinely Wuxing-orphan per R5. Restoring Mightyena to Earth Dog would require extending R5 the same way Poison-with-terrestrial-habitat extends to Earth. Reasonable extension but worth flagging — Dark types are nighttime / shadow / predator-coded, which is structurally similar to Poison-with-terrestrial: an adaptation, not a realm. Could justify the extension.

**Caveat on Vulpix:** Pure Fire (Kantonian) — element is on the card. Vulpix-Ninetales would fit Fire Dog. But Vulpix is famously kitsune (fox-spirit, supernatural lineage). Even under market test, kitsune carries strong cultural-category weight in Japan and East Asia. Open question whether Chinese collectors would accept Vulpix in Year of the Dog — Japanese fans definitely wouldn't (kitsune = 狐 / 狐仙 = sacred separate category in Japanese tradition too). Recommend keeping Vulpix orphaned even under market test, since kitsune crosses Japanese supernatural-category lines, not just Chinese.

**Status:** Recommended:
- Restore Lycanroc (Earth Dog), Mightyena (Earth Dog with R5 extension), Vaporeon (Water Dog)
- Keep Vulpix-Ninetales orphaned (kitsune is too strongly category-distinct, Japanese cultural alignment)
- Galar Wolves already restored (Crowned forms, Steel typing — passes both criteria already)
