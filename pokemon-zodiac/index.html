The 12 functional-agrarian slot criteria

The slot character's Chinese semantics dictate whether the criterion broadens (the character covers a family of similar animals) or stays strict (the character means that thing specifically). This is the structural ruling: lions and saber-tooths aren't tigers, the same way mice and rats are both rodents and sheep and goats are interchangeable.

  1. Rat (鼠) BROAD — small mammals with prominent incisors and a scampering body plan. Mice, rats, squirrels, beavers, hamsters, capybara-types. The agrarian pest broadly. Excludes shrews if not rodent-shaped, excludes rabbits (separate slot).
  2. Ox (牛) BROAD — 牛 covers all bovines: cattle, water buffalo (水牛), yak (牦牛), wild ox (野牛), bulls, oxen. The agrarian plow animal broadly. Per Bulbapedia, Terrakion is "bovine, quadrupedal" — passes 牛 cleanly. Bouffalant orphans via non-Chinese-fauna (American bison, wrong continent), not via slot-strictness.
  3. Tiger (虎) STRICT — actual tigers. Striped, solitary, predatory big cat with tiger silhouette. Excludes lions (Buddhist-imported iconography, separate Chinese category 狮 — Pyroar, Solgaleo, Entei, Suicune all orphaned). Excludes saber-tooths (Raikou orphaned), leopards/panthers, and all domestic-scaled cats per the Great Race myth. The character does not broaden the way 鼠 and 羊 do.
  4. Rabbit (兔) BROAD — lagomorph: long ears, hopping body plan, herbivore. Rabbits and hares. The moon-rabbit (玉兔) is canonical Chinese imagery — lunar / medicinal / agrarian.
  5. Dragon (龙) STRICT — Chinese loong specifically, plus the carp-dragon transformation carve-out (鯉躍龍門). Asian-loong-coded designs: serpentine, scaled, whiskered, four-clawed, sky/water-associated. Plus the carp-becomes-dragon lineage (Magikarp → Gyarados). Excludes Western heraldic dragons (Charizard line, winged fire-breathers). Excludes kaiju (Tyranitar line — Japanese tradition, not Chinese). The Dragon row collapses to Water Dragon only as a result.
  6. Snake (蛇) STRICT — legless or near-legless reptiles only. Slithering, coiling body. Eels are 鳗 (with the fish radical 魚) in Chinese tradition — they're fish, not snakes. Eelektross orphans to a separate category. Sea-serpents pass via visual coding only when not carp-dragon-coded (Milotic stays via sea-serpent visual, Gyarados goes to Dragon via carp-myth).
  7. Horse (马) STRICT — 马 means horse specifically. Donkeys are 驴, mules are 骡 — separate characters. Year of the Horse celebrates horses, not equines broadly. Mudbray reads donkey by visual coding, but the Mudbray-Mudsdale line stays via Mudsdale anchor (line-as-unit precedent, same as Mamoswine via Swinub). Zebras orphan via non-Chinese fauna — Blitzle/Zebstrika also pure Electric, doubly orphaned.
  8. Goat (羊) BROAD — horned ruminant: goat, sheep, or ram silhouette interchangeable. 山羊 (mountain 羊 = goat), 绵羊 (cotton 羊 = sheep). The character doesn't distinguish among them. Houndoom orphans structurally — decorative horns are not pastoral-ruminant horns; Houndoom is a hellhound, not a 羊. Stantler / Wyrdeer dropped (deer is 鹿, separate character).
  9. Monkey (猴) BROAD — primate: simian face, hands, climbing or knuckle-walking body. Apes, monkeys, lemurs, orangutans. The Sun Wukong (孫悟空) lineage is foundational — monkey-as-trickster-immortal. Excludes humans-as-monkeys (Mr. Mime — humanoid, separate category).
  10. Rooster (鸡) STRICT — chicken: galliform body, comb / wattle, dawn-crowing barnyard bird. The dex's massive avian variety is a structural mismatch with a one-bird zodiac slot. Skarmory, Dodrio, all raptors / songbirds / waterfowl / owls slot-orphaned.
  11. Dog (狗) STRICT — domestic dogs only. Companion / guardian / herding canid. Stoutland line, Arcanine line, Yamper line. Excludes wolves (狼 — wild predators, separate Chinese category — Mightyena, Lycanroc, Zacian / Zamazenta orphaned). Excludes foxes (狐仙 — fox-spirit lineage, separate supernatural category — Vulpix, Ninetales, Vaporeon orphaned). Excludes hyenas / jackals (non-Chinese fauna — Lucario orphaned despite jackal coding).
  12. Pig (猪) BROAD — suid: pig, boar, or wild swine silhouette. Snout, optional tusks. Pigs and boars. Mamoswine line passes via Swinub's pig coding (mammoth endpoint accepted as line-as-unit). Excludes hippos (Hippopotas), tapirs, peccaries-as-not-pigs.

Why so many open cells? — Tier 3 footnotes

Open cells are the most interesting cells. They show where the Pokémon dex went Western, Japanese, or fantastical instead of reaching into Chinese tradition. Honored gaps, not failures.

Domestic cats — zodiac-orphaned per the Great Race myth (Rat tricked Cat off the Ox; cat-rat enmity is canonical). Persian, Espeon, Skitty, Litten line, Sprigatito, Meowth all slot-orphaned.

Foxes (狐仙) — supernatural fox-spirit lineage in Chinese folklore, distinct from domestic-dog category. Vulpix, Ninetales, Zorua/Zoroark, Fennekin/Delphox, Nickit, Vaporeon all slot-orphaned.

Wolves (狼) — wild predators, antagonist of the shepherd in agrarian symbolism, not a domestic-companion canid. Mightyena, Lycanroc, Zacian/Zamazenta all slot-orphaned.

Lions, saber-tooths, panthers — not tigers — 虎 means tiger specifically. Unlike 鼠 (which broadens to any rodent under R1) and 羊 (which broadens to goat/sheep/ram under R2), the tiger character does not broaden. A lion is not a tiger the way a mouse IS a rodent or a goat IS a sheep. Lions also carry separate Chinese symbolism (狮) — Buddhist-imported iconography from guardian-lion statues and lion dance, not native fauna. Pyroar, Solgaleo, Entei, Suicune (lions), Hisuian Arcanine (lion-tiger), Raikou (saber-tooth) all slot-orphaned. Tiger row goes fully open — the dex has plenty of big cats but no actual striped tigers.

Most birds — Rooster slot is chicken-specific in mainstream Chinese tradition; the Pokémon dex's massive avian variety is a structural mismatch with a zodiac that allotted one bird slot to the domestic chicken. Skarmory, Dodrio, all raptors/songbirds/waterfowl/owls slot-orphaned.

Pure Electric — Wu Xing has no Air element. Lightning is air-coded in Pokémon (Zapdos Electric/Flying, Tornadus/Thundurus paired wind-lightning genies). Wu Xing's Metal is substantive (refinement, structure, blade), not kinetic. Pikachu, Mareep line, Pawmi, Yamper line all element-orphaned.

Pure Flying / Psychic / Dark / Fairy / Ghost — no Five-Phases equivalent. Element-orphaned even when animal-slot eligible.

Western dragons — winged fire-breathers, distinct from Chinese loong (serpentine, whiskered, four-clawed, sky/water-associated). Charizard line, Reshiram, Turtonator, Volcarona slot-orphaned despite Dragon-type or fire-bug coding.

Kaiju — Japanese mythological tradition, not Chinese. Tyranitar line slot-orphaned. The Dragon row tightens to Water Dragon only — Kingdra (seahorse-loong), Palkia, Gyarados (the carp-dragon transformation, 鯉躍龍門), Dragalge.

Donkeys (驴) — separate character from 马 (horse) in Chinese; mules are 骡, also separate. Mudbray reads donkey by visual coding. Mudbray-Mudsdale line stays in Earth Horse via Mudsdale anchor (line-as-unit precedent — same logic as Mamoswine via Swinub).

Deer (鹿) — separate character from 羊 (goat / sheep / ram). Antlers are not pastoral-ruminant horns; deer carry their own Chinese cultural symbolism (longevity, immortality). Stantler / Wyrdeer dropped from Earth Goat under strict 羊 reading.

Eels (鳗) — written with the fish radical 魚 in Chinese; eels are fish in Chinese tradition, not snakes (蛇). Eelektross orphans from Water Snake under strict 蛇 reading.

Non-Chinese fauna — zebras (African — Blitzle / Zebstrika), American bison (Bouffalant — wrong continent), jackals (Egyptian / Mediterranean — Lucario), mammoths / hippos / tapirs all slot-orphaned even when type-honest.

Houndoom specifically — orphans from both the Goat row (decorative horns aren't pastoral-ruminant horns) AND the Dog row (hellhound, not a domestic companion 狗).
