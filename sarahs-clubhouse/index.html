~*~★ OFFICIAL GAME REVIEW CORNER ★~*~

Mary-Kate and Ashley: Magical Mystery Mall (PS1)

★★★★★★★★★★ / 5

(yes thats 10 out of 5. the scale wasnt big enough. deal w it.)

OK SO. if u havent played Mary-Kate and Ashley: Magical Mystery Mall yet then like WHAT R U EVEN DOING WITH UR LIFE??? My mom got me this 4 christmas and i literally SCREAMED so loud the dog started barking hahahaha. So basically MK&A get these enchanted necklaces that put a SPELL on the whole mall and freeze everyone in time (creepy but also kinda cool??) and u have 2 collect all 5 magical gems 2 break the curse and escape. its giving fantasy adventure but make it FASHION u kno??

Theres like 5 different levels u can do in any order which is amazing. Fash-Level is OBVIOUSLY the best one, u put together outfits and do a runway walk and take photos and theres even a secret Tiki Hut runway u can unlock (i figured that out by myself btw no walkthrough needed ty very much). The Dance Video stage is sooo fun, it says RIGHT on the cover "Dance moves by Americas #1 Music Video Choreographer" like HELLO thats basically professional dancing on ur PS1?? me and my BFF used 2 practice all the choreography in my living room and my dad would walk by like "r u guys ok" and wed be like "DAD UR RUINING OUR REHEARSAL GO AWAY" lolol. Theres also SNOWBOARDING inside the mall somehow??? and Lizzie's Lunch where u serve food which is lowkey the hardest mini game ive ever played in my LIFE i literally almost threw my controller. oh and the Bonus Boutique has a SLOT MACHINE where u spend tokens which is probably not appropriate 4 kids but whatever lol its fun.

The only bad thing is sometimes the controls r kinda weird and Mary-Kate walks like shes on an ice rink lol. Also IGN gave this game a 4 out of 10 which is literally a CRIME against journalism and honestly whoever wrote that review has never known joy. Also my PS1 freezes sometimes but thats bc my brother spilled mountain dew on it at his stupid sleepover.
