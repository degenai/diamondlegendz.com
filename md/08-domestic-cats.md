# Case 08 — Domestic cats (Litten, Persian, Skitty, Sprigatito) — no zodiac slot

**Question:** Domestic-scale cats are universally beloved Pokémon designs (Litten line is a Gen 7 starter, Sprigatito is a Gen 9 starter, Persian is iconic). Should they have a zodiac home under any reading?

## The structural problem

Chinese zodiac canon does not include domestic cats. The Great Race myth is canonical: the Jade Emperor invited animals to a race; Rat tricked Cat off the back of Ox, Cat missed the race, no zodiac slot. The cat's anger at the rat is mythologized as the canonical reason cats and rats are enemies in Chinese folklore.

Vietnamese zodiac substitutes Rabbit with Cat, but per the project's R0 China-max stance (no Vietnamese variant), this substitution is rejected.

**Result: domestic cats have no zodiac home in the grid. Period.**

## Affected Pokémon

- **Litten / Torracat / Incineroar** — Fire (with Fire/Dark at final stage). Heel-wrestler tiger lore at Incineroar but base form is house cat.
- **Persian / Meowth (all forms)** — house cat
- **Skitty / Delcatty** — house cat
- **Sprigatito line** — Gen 9 cat starter (Floragato, Meowscarada)
- **Espeon / Umbreon / Liepard** — cat-coded eeveelutions / leopard
- **Glameow / Purugly** — house cat
- **Vivillon-adjacent / Sneasel / Weavile** — varied cat-influenced designs

## The Litten / Incineroar exception (line-as-unit precedent)

Incineroar is *explicitly* tiger-coded by Bulbapedia: "Incineroar is a heel wrestler tiger-themed Pokémon." Per the line-as-unit precedent established in the Pig row (Mamoswine line passes Pig via Swinub-as-pig anchor despite mammoth endpoint) and Horse row (Mudbray-Mudsdale line passes Horse via Mudsdale anchor despite Mudbray-as-donkey base), the Litten line could potentially pass Tiger row via Incineroar's tiger endpoint coding.

**However:** Tiger row is fully open under strict 虎 reading regardless of Litten line. Even if Litten line passes via Incineroar tiger-coding, this places Litten in **Fire Tiger** specifically.

The earlier ruling: Litten line orphaned via "cat-coded" because Litten and Torracat are domestic-cat-scale at base, even though Incineroar is tiger-scale. The decision turns on whether the line-as-unit precedent applies to *animal* fit (where the endpoint dictates) or whether each card is judged independently for animal slot.

The precedent is established for cases where the endpoint slot fits AND the mid-stages don't break the slot semantically. Mamoswine works because mammoth is "giant pig + ice fur" — close enough. Mudbray-Mudsdale works because Mudbray is "donkey-shaped" but still equine. Litten-Torracat-Incineroar would work if "Litten is tiger-cub-coded enough to pass Tiger slot." That's contested — Litten is more house-kitten than tiger-cub.

## Market evidence

No TPC zodiac event has explicitly featured house cats for Year of the Tiger 2022 — the Litten-line wasn't bundled. TPC respected the Great Race myth orphan, even though Western fans would absolutely accept Incineroar in a Year-of-the-Tiger box.

**However:** Year of the Tiger 2022 Pokémon merchandise in Japan and the West did sometimes feature Incineroar as a "tiger" representative — this is the market reality even though strict Chinese tradition orphans cats. The tension here is: Chinese-specific market vs global Pokémon collector market.

## Recommendation

**Maintain cat orphan under Chinese-specific market reading.** The Great Race myth is too canonical to override; Chinese collectors specifically would not accept a cat-base Litten in a Year-of-the-Tiger box, even if Western collectors would.

**Possible Litten-line exception via Incineroar-as-tiger endpoint** — under the line-as-unit precedent. This is genuinely contested and worth flagging:

- *Pro restoration*: Incineroar is unambiguously tiger-coded by Bulbapedia. The line ends as tiger. Same precedent as Mamoswine (mammoth endpoint accepted as part of the pig line).
- *Against restoration*: Litten itself is too clearly house-kitten. Mamoswine's base Swinub IS a pig; Litten is a kitten, not a tiger cub. The base-form-fits requirement matters.

**Status:** Cats orphaned. The Litten-line-via-Incineroar question stays open as a future case worth debating; for now Tiger row stays fully empty per strict reading.
