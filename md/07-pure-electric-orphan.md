# Case 07 — Pure Electric (Mareep, Pikachu, Yamper) — element-orphan rule

**Question:** Pure Electric Pokémon are popular and zodiac-relevant by animal coding (Mareep is sheep, Pikachu is mouse, Yamper is corgi), but they have no Wuxing element home. Does the market test override the R5 element-orphan rule?

## The structural problem

Wu Xing has five phases: Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water. There is no Air or Lightning phase. Pokémon's Electric-type is consistently *atmospheric* in its lore — Zapdos is Electric/Flying, Tornadus and Thundurus are paired wind/lightning genie spirits, Raikou's signature is "speed of lightning across the plains." Electricity is a *kinetic atmospheric phenomenon* in Pokémon, not an elemental realm.

Wu Xing's Metal (金) is *substantive*: refinement, structure, blade, finished forged matter — explicitly NOT kinetic. Forcing Electric → Metal would conflict with the metaphysics of Wu Xing.

Result: pure Electric Pokémon are Wuxing-element-orphans, even when they fit a zodiac animal slot perfectly.

## Affected Pokémon

- **Mareep / Flaaffy / Ampharos** — sheep-coded (Goat slot), pure Electric
- **Pikachu / Pichu / Raichu** — mouse-coded (Rat slot), pure Electric
- **Yamper / Boltund** — corgi-coded (Dog slot), pure Electric
- **Pawmi / Pawmot** — mouse-coded (Rat slot), pure Electric
- **Blitzle / Zebstrika** — zebra-coded (Horse slot adjacent — also doubly orphaned via non-Chinese fauna)
- **Electrike / Manectric** — dog-coded, pure Electric

## Market evidence

**Mareep / Flaaffy were widely treated as Year of the Sheep 2015 Pokémon** in fan content — see [PaperPokés — Flaaffy Chinese New Year 2015](http://www.pokemonpapercraft.net/2010/08/flaaffy-zodiac-2015-sheep.html), an officially-style papercraft for Year of the Sheep. The animal-fit was unambiguous to fans; the element wasn't part of the consideration.

**Pokémon GO Year of the Dog 2018 explicitly featured Electrike** as one of the canid-coded Pokémon for the event. Source: [Inverse — Year of the Dog Lunar New Year](https://www.inverse.com/article/41374-pokemon-go-year-of-the-dog-lunar-new-year). TPC didn't filter by element.

**Pikachu transcends zodiac.** Per nearly every Pokémon Center Lunar New Year collection (2022, 2023, 2024, 2025), Pikachu appears in a "Lunar New Year Costume" plush regardless of the zodiac animal of the year. Pikachu is the brand mascot, not a zodiac anchor. Source: [Pokémon Center — Lunar New Year Pikachu plush](https://www.pokemoncenter.com/product/701E12095/lunar-new-year-costume-pikachu-female-plush-9-in).

## The grid's hard constraint

The 60-cell grid is structured as 12 zodiac animals × 5 Wuxing elements. Every cell *must* have an element. Pure Electric doesn't map to any of the 5 elements without breaking R5 metaphysics. This isn't a market-test problem — it's a structural problem.

A collector building a Year of the Sheep box can absolutely include Mareep and Flaaffy. A collector looking at the grid wanting "Wood Sheep" or "Fire Sheep" or "Metal Sheep" specifically won't find Mareep there because Mareep doesn't have a Wuxing-coded element.

## Recommendation

**Maintain the R5 pure-Electric element-orphan rule.** Add a clarifying footnote in Tier 3 acknowledging:

> Pure Electric Pokémon (Pikachu, Mareep line, Yamper, Pawmi, Electrike, Blitzle) animal-fit cleanly into their zodiac rows but have no Wuxing element home. The market would accept them in any Year-of-X collection for their animal slot — Mareep for Year of the Sheep is canonical fan-tradition — but the grid's Wuxing-element row structure cannot accommodate them. Electric is atmospheric/kinetic in Pokémon's cosmology; Wuxing's Metal is substantive (refinement, blade, structure), not kinetic. The orphan is structural, not market-based.

**Suggestion:** The page could include a separate "Animal-row companions" section listing zodiac-relevant pure-Electric (and pure-Flying/Psychic/Dark/Fairy/Ghost) Pokémon as honorable mentions for collectors building animal-themed boxes that don't slice by Wuxing element. They're not on the grid but they're recognized as part of the row's collector universe.

**Status:** Pure Electric stays orphaned. Recommend adding a "honorable mentions" companion list to the Tier 3 footnotes for collectors who don't care about Wuxing-element slicing.
