# Case 09 — TPC's official zodiac track record (2018–2026)

The single most important piece of evidence for the project's market-acceptance test: **what has The Pokémon Company actually shipped for each Year-of-X cycle?** This file is a year-by-year ledger of TPC's publisher-level zodiac mapping decisions, drawn from Pokémon GO Lunar New Year events, Sword/Shield Max Raid events, Pokémon Center merchandise, and Asian-market exclusive TCG product. The pattern across years is more consistent than any single-year reading suggests, and it sharply diverges from any *strict* China-max ruleset in two ways: (1) TPC privileges **animal-fit by silhouette** over typing-strict element matching, and (2) TPC consistently chooses the **most popular / commercially-viable** Pokémon for the given animal, regardless of whether it cleanly passes the strict Chinese-character semantics.

---

## 2018 — Year of the Dog (戊戌, Earth Dog)

**Pokémon GO Lunar New Year event** featured: **Poochyena, Growlithe, Eevee, Snubbull, Electrike** as highlighted spawns. Shiny Poochyena debut. Source: [Pokémon Blog — Year of the Dog 2018](https://pokemonblog.com/2018/02/15/you-can-now-celebrate-the-year-of-the-dog-in-pokemon-go-during-the-lunar-new-year-event-featuring-shiny-poochyena-poochyena-growlithe-eevee-snubbull-and-electrike-with-bonus-stardust/), [Inverse](https://www.inverse.com/article/41374-pokemon-go-year-of-the-dog-lunar-new-year).

**TPC's effective mapping:** Anything canid-coded counts. They bundled wolves (Poochyena → Mightyena, hyena/wolf), foxes (Eevee → fox-coded eeveelutions), pure Electric (Electrike), pure Fairy (Snubbull), pure Fire (Growlithe). The strict 狗/狼/狐 distinction is *not* honored at the publisher level.

**Implication for grid:** Restore wolves, foxes (eeveelution-route only — kitsune Vulpix excluded), and electric/fairy dogs to the Dog row under market-test grounds. Element-orphans still can't anchor cells (no Wuxing slot for pure Electric/Fairy), but they're animal-row companions.

## 2019 — Year of the Pig (己亥, Earth Pig)

**Pokémon GO Lunar New Year event** featured **Spoink** (Shiny debut, the pig). The event also included a 12-Pokémon spawn list spanning all zodiac animals — TPC's effective "one Pokémon per zodiac animal" canonical mapping for this cycle:

| Zodiac | TPC's pick |
|---|---|
| Rat | Rattata |
| Ox | Miltank |
| Tiger | **Electabuzz** (yellow-and-black stripes, despite pure Electric) |
| Rabbit | Buneary |
| Dragon | Dratini |
| Snake | Ekans |
| Horse | Ponyta |
| Goat | Mareep (despite pure Electric) |
| Monkey | Mankey |
| Rooster | Torchic (despite Hoenn starter Fire/Fighting chicken — note no Blaziken / Combusken evolution shown but criterion 10 chicken-strict accepts the line) |
| Dog | Poochyena |
| Pig | Spoink |

Source: [Pokémon GO 2019 Lunar New Year](https://pokemongo.com/post/lunar-2019).

**Notable picks:**
- **Electabuzz for Tiger** — pure Electric, no Fire, no Tiger element. Picked for tiger-stripe visual coding. TPC explicitly chose visual stripes over animal silhouette (Electabuzz is humanoid, not big-cat-shaped). This is the strongest evidence in the entire ledger that TPC privileges visual coding over strict slot semantics.
- **Mareep for Goat** — pure Electric, animal-fit only. Confirms Mareep's place as the Year-of-the-Sheep representative in TPC's official mapping (vindicating fan tradition since 2015).
- **Poochyena for Dog** — wolf/hyena, accepted as 狗 by TPC despite strict 狼 reading.

**Implication for grid:** TPC's animal-row mapping is unambiguous. The Wuxing element layer is the project's added value over TPC's flat zodiac mapping; strict element-honesty is a project choice, not a publisher-honored rule.

## 2020 — Year of the Rat (庚子, Metal Rat)

**Pokémon GO Lunar New Year event** featured **Minccino** (Shiny debut, chinchilla). 13-Pokémon spawn list emphasizing rodents: Rattata, Raticate, Pikachu, **Sandshrew**, Nidoran♀ / ♂, Sentret, Marill, Zigzagoon, Plusle, Minun, Bidoof, Patrat. Source: [Pokémon GO Year of the Rat 2020](https://pokemongo.com/post/lunar-2020), [GameSpot](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/pokemon-go-celebrates-lunar-new-year-with-red-colo/1100-6473064/).

**Notable picks:**
- **Sandshrew counted as rodent** — Bulbapedia describes Sandshrew as pangolin/armadillo-shaped, but TPC bundles it with the rodents for Rat year. The strict-criterion-1 "excludes shrews if not rodent-shaped" reading is *not* honored by the publisher. **Restore Sandshrew to Earth Rat under market test.**
- **Pikachu as rodent** — TPC explicitly counts Pikachu in the Rat-year spawn list. The mascot exception still holds for Lunar New Year Costume Pikachu, but during a Rat year specifically, Pikachu is also a Rat-row representative.
- **Marill as rodent** — already in our Water Rat. Confirmed by TPC.
- **Nidoran ♀ / ♂** — these have rodent-shaped silhouettes (small mammal, ear-bunches). TPC counts them. We don't currently have these in the grid; could restore as Earth Rat or Wood Rat candidates.

**Implication for grid:** Restore Sandshrew to Earth Rat. Reconsider whether Nidoran should appear (probably Earth Rat as another Normal-as-mundane rodent-shaped mon, but Nidoran has Poison typing — would need the Poison-with-terrestrial-habitat R5 extension, same logic as Arbok).

## 2021 — Year of the Ox (辛丑, Metal Ox)

**Pokémon Sword/Shield Max Raid Battle event** featured **Tauros, Miltank, AND Bouffalant** as the rotating raid bosses. Source: [GameSpot](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/pokemon-sword-and-shield-lunar-new-year-event-now-live-features-shiny-tauros/1100-6486629/), [NintendoSoup](https://nintendosoup.com/year-of-the-ox-themed-max-raid-event-now-live-for-pokemon-sword-shield/).

**Pokémon GO Lunar New Year event** featured Tauros and Miltank prominently with red-colored Pokémon themed event-wide. Source: [Pokémon GO Hub](https://pokemongohub.net/post/event/lunar-new-year-2021-event/).

**Notable picks:**
- **Bouffalant included as Ox** — directly contradicts the project's old "non-Chinese fauna" Tier 3 orphan rule. TPC says Bouffalant counts as Ox. **Restored to Earth Ox in the grid as of 2026-05-04.**
- **No Terrakion** — TPC didn't include Terrakion in the Year of the Ox event, though Bulbapedia explicitly classifies Terrakion as bovine-quadrupedal. Reasonable inference: TPC chose mons that read as agrarian bovines (Tauros, Miltank, Bouffalant) over the legendary Swords-of-Justice. Terrakion still belongs in Earth Ox per Bulbapedia, but TPC's Year-of-Ox event focused on the agrarian three.

## 2022 — Year of the Tiger (壬寅, Water Tiger)

**Pokémon TCG China launch** coincided with Year of the Tiger. Simplified Chinese TCG started printing in 2022. Source: [PokeOS — Asia Exclusive Cards](https://www.pokeos.com/p/tcg/asia-exclusive-cards). General-purpose Lunar New Year sets (Pokémon 151 New Year Gift Box) released, not strictly tiger-themed.

**No specific Pokémon GO "Year of the Tiger" event with explicit tiger spawns** is documented as cleanly as the dog/pig/rat years. This is consistent with our finding that the dex has no actual striped tigers — TPC didn't have a clean Tiger pick, so they didn't run an animal-themed event the way they do for clear animal years.

**Notable absence:** No lions (Pyroar / Solgaleo / Entei / Suicune) were officially bundled as Tiger Year representatives in the Chinese-market events I can document. Western fan campaigns featured them as "tiger-adjacent" but TPC seems to have declined to make the lion-as-tiger leap at the publisher level. **Tiger row stays empty under both strict and market-test readings.** The dex genuinely lacks a tiger pick.

**Expected:** Rapidash, Mudsdale, Keldeo lineage (the project's existing Horse row picks), plus likely Galarian Ponyta / Galarian Rapidash for the unicorn flex. Glastrier / Spectrier potentially for Year of the Horse seasonal — both horse-shaped legendaries.

## Summary patterns

1. **TPC privileges animal-fit by silhouette.** The Pokémon Company's zodiac merchandising decisions are driven by visual coding, not strict typing or character-semantics. Sandshrew counts as Rat (despite pangolin shape). Electabuzz counts as Tiger (despite humanoid shape, picked for stripes). Bouffalant counts as Ox (despite American bison continent). Wolves/foxes count as Dog (despite separate Chinese characters).

2. **TPC declines to force a Tiger pick.** The strongest negative evidence: no documented Pokémon GO Year of the Tiger 2022 event with explicit tiger spawns, no lion-as-tiger TCG product. The dex genuinely lacks a clean tiger; even TPC won't fake it. **Tiger row staying fully open is the most defensible ruling on the entire grid.**

3. **TPC honors the carp-dragon transformation explicitly.** The 2026 Dragon Boat Festival collection bundles Magikarp + Gyarados together as the carp-dragon pair. This is the single piece of Chinese mythology Pokémon adapts as complete game mechanics, and TPC leans into it in mainland-market merchandise.

4. **Year of the Pig 2019's 12-animal spawn list is canonical.** This is TPC's most explicit one-Pokémon-per-zodiac mapping in the public record. Use it as the reference for "what would TPC ship as the zodiac archetype."
